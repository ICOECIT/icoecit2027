Refund & Cancellation Policy

Please read this policy carefully before making any registration payment for ICoECIT 2027.

⚠️

Registration fees are non-refundable

All registration fees paid to ICoECIT 2027 are non-refundable and non-transferable, except in the limited circumstances described below. By completing payment you acknowledge and accept this policy.

1. General principle

Conference registration reserves your place in the technical programme and covers per-paper publication and organizational costs that are committed in advance. For this reason, fees once paid are not refundable and cannot be transferred to another person, paper or a future edition, regardless of attendance.

2. No refund for the following

  • Change of mind, inability to attend, or failure to present (“no-show”).
  • Paper withdrawal after registration, or rejection due to plagiarism/policy violation.
  • Visa refusal, travel disruption, or personal/medical reasons.
  • Duplicate or excess payments arising from author error (see §4 for genuine gateway errors).
  • Taxes, bank charges and payment-gateway fees, which are never refundable.

3. If the conference is cancelled or postponed by the organizers

Because ICoECIT 2027 is still under review with the publisher, please note:

  • Registration will normally open only after the conference is approved. If the organizers open registration and the event is subsequently cancelled by the organizers, registrants will be offered a refund of the base registration fee, less non-recoverable taxes and payment-gateway charges.
  • If the event is postponed or its mode changed (e.g. to online/hybrid), registrations remain valid for the rescheduled event; no separate refund is due.
  • Refunds, where applicable, are the organizers’ maximum liability and are limited to the amount actually received.

4. Duplicate / erroneous transactions

If you are charged twice for the same registration due to a genuine payment-gateway error, the duplicate amount (less unrecoverable gateway charges) will be refunded. Report such cases within 7 days of the transaction with proof of payment.

5. How to raise a request

Eligible refund requests must be emailed to icoecitconferencesecetary@gmail.com with the registration ID, payment reference and reason. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 15–30 working days, subject to gateway timelines.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when registration formally opens. The version in force at the time of your payment applies. This policy is read together with our Terms & Conditions.
