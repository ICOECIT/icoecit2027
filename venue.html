Venue

Hyderabad, India

ICoECIT 2027 is proposed to be hosted on the campus of G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS), Hyderabad — the same venue as the inaugural 2026 edition.

G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science

Department of Information Technology (host department)

GNITS is an autonomous institute affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad, and is accredited by NAAC and NBA. The IT department specialises in technical education and research in emerging areas including AI, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, Blockchain and IoT.

The campus features modern infrastructure with state-of-the-art laboratories and smart classrooms, and hosts active student chapters including IEEE, ACM and CSI. Departmental research is supported by DST, AICTE and UGC.

Getting there

  • Air: Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (HYD), ~35 km — taxis and app cabs available.
  • Rail: Hyderabad Deccan (Nampally) & Secunderabad Junction are the nearest major stations.
  • Metro / Road: Well connected within Hyderabad by metro, bus and app cabs; located in the Shaikpet neighbourhood.

Explore Hyderabad

Delegates can visit Charminar, Golconda Fort, the Salar Jung Museum, Ramoji Film City and HITEC City. Visa guidance for international delegates will be provided closer to the event.
