Venue

Patna, Bihar, India

ICoECIT 2027 is tentatively proposed to be held on 28–29 January 2027 at Taj City Centre, Patna. The conference is organized by G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS), Hyderabad.

⚠️

Venue is tentative

The venue below is proposed and subject to confirmation, and depends on the outcome of the ongoing publisher review. Final venue arrangements will be published here once confirmed.

Taj City Centre, Patna

Proposed conference venue

Taj City Centre, Patna is a contemporary business hotel located in the heart of the city on Buddh Marg, offering modern conferencing facilities, banquet spaces and hospitality suited to an international academic event. Its central location provides convenient access to the airport, railway stations and the city's business and leisure districts.

The conference is organized and financially sponsored by G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS), Hyderabad — an autonomous institute affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and accredited by NAAC and NBA, with active IEEE, ACM and CSI student chapters.

Getting there

  • Air: Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport (PAT), Patna — approx. 6.5 km (~20 min) from the venue.
  • Rail: Patna Junction (~2.3 km) and Rajendra Nagar Terminal (~5 km) are the nearest major railway stations.
  • Road / Local: Centrally located on Buddh Marg; well served by auto-rickshaws, app cabs and city buses.

Explore Patna

Delegates can visit Golghar, the Bihar Museum and Patna Museum, Takht Sri Patna Sahib (Harmandir), Mahavir Mandir, the ancient site of Kumhrar and Gandhi Maidan. Nalanda and Bodh Gaya make popular day trips.
