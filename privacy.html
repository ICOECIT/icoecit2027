Privacy Policy

How the ICoECIT 2027 secretariat collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: this policy applies to the ICoECIT 2027 website and registration process. Effective date to be confirmed at launch.

1. Who we are

ICoECIT 2027 (the “Conference”) is organized by G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS), Shaikpet, Hyderabad, Telangana 500104, India (“we”, “us”, “the organizers”). This policy explains how we handle personal data collected through this website and the registration/submission process.

2. Information we collect

  • Author & delegate details — name, affiliation, designation, postal address, email and phone number.
  • Submission data — manuscript title, abstract, author list and the paper file you upload.
  • Registration & payment data — category, IEEE membership details (if claimed) and transaction references. Card/bank details are processed directly by our payment gateway and are not stored by us.
  • Technical data — basic analytics such as browser type and pages visited, used only to improve the website.

3. How we use your information

  • To manage paper submission, peer review, acceptance and publication of the proceedings.
  • To process registration and payments and issue certificates.
  • To communicate important conference announcements, schedules and changes.
  • To comply with the publisher’s requirements should the conference be approved for publication.

4. Sharing of information

We share personal data only as necessary with: members of the Technical Program and Organizing Committees for review purposes; the payment gateway for processing transactions; and, only if and when the conference is approved by the publisher, with IEEE for publication and indexing of accepted papers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data retention

We retain submission and registration data for the duration necessary to run the conference and to meet publication and statutory record-keeping obligations, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by writing to the secretariat. Requests will be handled subject to publication and legal obligations.

7. Cookies

This website may use minimal cookies and third-party fonts to function and to measure basic traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the conference progresses (including once its publication status is confirmed). The latest version will always be posted on this page.

10. Contact

For any privacy question, contact the Conference Secretary, Dr. A. Vijay Krishna, at icoecitconferencesecetary@gmail.com.
