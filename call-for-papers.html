Call for Papers

Share your research at ICoECIT 2027

ICoECIT 2027 invites high-quality original research contributions across computing, electronics, communication, security and smart technologies. All submissions undergo a rigorous peer-review process.

⚠️

Please read before submitting

The application for IEEE technical co-sponsorship and publication of ICoECIT 2027 proceedings is under review with the publisher and has not yet been approved. Indexing and inclusion in IEEE Xplore cannot be guaranteed until the conference is formally approved and an agreement is signed. Authors submit on this understanding. Formatting requirements below follow the standard IEEE conference template so that manuscripts are ready should approval be granted.

How to Submit

Submission guidelines

Follow these steps to prepare and submit your manuscript.

  1. Format your paper using the standard IEEE conference (double-column, A4) template — download from the IEEE templates page.
  2. Keep within the page limit — maximum 10 pages including references (one extra page permitted with an additional charge).
  3. Check originality — manuscripts must be original and will be screened for plagiarism and AI-generated content per IEEE policy.
  4. Submit electronically via the conference submission portal (Microsoft CMT / EDAS link to be announced once the conference is approved).
  5. Peer review — each paper is reviewed by the Technical Program Committee.
  6. Camera-ready & registration — on acceptance, submit the camera-ready manuscript and complete registration for the paper to be included.

Author policies

ICoECIT follows IEEE publication principles:

  • Plagiarism policy — zero tolerance; all papers checked with similarity software.
  • Publication ethics — no duplicate submission, fabrication or falsification.
  • No-show policy — accepted papers not presented may be excluded from publication.
  • Non-refundable fees — registration fees are non-transferable/non-refundable once paid.
