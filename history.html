About the 2026 → 2027 transition

Publication by IEEE for the 2026 edition does not automatically extend to 2027. Each edition requires its own approval. The 2027 application is under review; until it is approved and an agreement is signed, ICoECIT 2027 makes no claim of IEEE sponsorship, and IEEE indexing of the 2027 proceedings cannot be guaranteed.
