Registration

Register for ICoECIT 2027

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the work for it to be included in the proceedings. Registration fees are listed below; payment is completed only through the official payment-gateway link sent in the acceptance-notification email.

⚠️

How registration works

ICoECIT 2027 is still under review with the publisher, so amounts remain subject to confirmation. Registration opens after acceptance and can only be completed via the official payment-gateway link in the acceptance-notification email — payments made through any other channel are considered null and void.

Page limit & extra-page charges

  • The registration fee covers a manuscript of up to 6 pages (excluding references).
  • Maximum permissible length is 10 pages (including references).
  • Extra pages are charged at ₹500 per page if paid by the regular registration deadline, or ₹800 per page if paid after it.
  • IEEE members must upload a valid IEEE membership card to avail the discounted fee.

Certificates

  • One presenting author (only one, where multiple authors present) receives a complimentary participation certificate; no more than one name can appear on it.
  • Additional participation certificates for presenting authors: ₹500 each by the regular deadline (₹800 each after).
  • Authors who do not present are issued a Contribution certificate at ₹500 each by the regular deadline (₹800 each after).
⚠️

Payment & submission rules

Registration can only be done using the official payment-gateway link mentioned in the acceptance-notification email — payment done through any other channel will be considered null and void. Papers can be submitted only through the official Microsoft CMT portal; submissions by any other means will be considered a desk reject. Bank / payment-gateway charges and taxes apply on the actual amount as per financial-agency / Government norms. Fees are non-transferable and non-refundable once paid — see our Refund Policy.
