Registration

Register for ICoECIT 2027

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the work for it to be included in the proceedings. The registration portal and final fees will be published after the conference is approved.

⚠️

Registration not yet open

Because ICoECIT 2027 is still under review with the publisher, the registration portal is not live. The fee table below is indicative only and will be finalised — along with IEEE-member discounts — once approval is confirmed.

What registration includes

  • Presentation slot and inclusion in the technical programme
  • Inclusion in the proceedings (subject to publisher approval)
  • Conference kit and certificate of presentation / participation
  • Access to keynote and technical sessions

Registration terms

  • Paper limited to 10 pages including one extra page (extra-page charges apply).
  • IEEE members must upload a valid IEEE membership card to avail the discounted fee.
  • Bank charges / taxes / payment-gateway fees apply as per actuals.
  • Fees are non-transferable and non-refundable once paid — see our Refund Policy.
